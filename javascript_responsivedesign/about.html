About Flowers & Decor services

It's all about finding the calm beauty in a chaos

Flowers & Decor is a floral and event design agency for weddings and special occasions. Our company offers a fresh and attentive approach to wedding and event decor. We are able to accommodate weddings of all sizes and offer the same attention to detail whether you are hosting an intimate celebration or a party with 500 guests. Our services include but are not limited to:

  • Concept and theme production
  • Decor and floral
  • Candle treatments and custom decor installations
  • Layout and floorplan creation
  • Bouquets and personal flowers
